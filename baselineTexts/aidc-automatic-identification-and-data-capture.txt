ISB 0108: AIDC: Automatic Identification and Data Capture

The AIDC: Automatic Identification and Data Capture (AIDC) standard provides the specification and guidance for achieving accurate AIDC across the NHS.

About this information standard

This standard applies to any NHS setting in England where automatic identification and data capture (AIDC) technologies are used as enablers to improve patient safety, ensure greater clinical effectiveness and drive operational efficiencies. It should be used by all NHS organisations when procuring, implementing and using such technologies.
